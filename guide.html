Hospital Classification & Symptom Guide

Primary Care

Health centers & clinics. Good for minor ailments, preventive care, simple consultations.

  • Cold & Flu
  • Minor cuts
  • Vaccinations

Secondary Care

District hospitals. Better for specialist consultations, basic surgery, maternity.

  • Broken bones
  • Moderate abdominal pain
  • Childbirth

Tertiary Care

Large medical centers. Reserved for severe trauma, ICU, and complex surgeries.

  • Heart attack / Stroke
  • Severe bleeding
  • Major accidents
Symptom Recommended Facility Action
Mild Fever & Cough Primary Care / Telehealth Find Telehealth
Suspected Fracture Secondary Care Find Hospital
Chest Pain / Difficulty Breathing Tertiary Care (ER) Call Philippines Standard 911
